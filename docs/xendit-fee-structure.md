## Rekomendasi Service Fee per Channel

Agar biaya Xendit tidak dimakan margin, set service fee per channel:

| Channel | Fee Xendit | Service Fee Customer | Keterangan |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|------------|
| QRIS | 0.63% | **1%** | Sudah cover + sedikit margin |
| Virtual Account | Rp 4.000 flat | **Rp 4.500** | Flat fee ke customer |
| GoPay | 5% | **5.5%** | Mahal — pertimbangkan tidak diaktifkan |
| OVO/DANA/ShopeePay | 1.5–1.8% | **2%** | Aman |

> 💡 **Saran:** Aktifkan QRIS + OVO + DANA saja. VA opsional. GoPay mahal banget untuk order kecil.
